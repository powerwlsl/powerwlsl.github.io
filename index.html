Welcome to Shiba Inu World!

1. Why Shiba?

The Shiba Inu is very challenging to raise and train. A bold, high-spirited "big dog in a small body," he must always be kept on-leash, for he has a high prey drive and quick reflexes and will pursue anything that moves.

2. Shiba is ..

  • sturdy, strong and conveniently-sized.
  • smart and clever
  • energetic, bold and spirited
